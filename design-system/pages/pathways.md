# Design Override: Pathways Pages

> Extends MASTER.md for Phase 5 — Prison Leavers & Homelessness Pathways
> Audience: Vulnerable users (prison leavers, homeless, people in recovery)
> Tone: Empathetic, plain-language, reassuring — not clinical

## Overrides / Additions

### Background
- Page background: `bg-[#FAFAF8]` (same as master — warm, not clinical)
- Hero band: `bg-blue-800 text-white` with amber bottom accent line

### Typography
- Body copy: `text-base leading-loose` (more generous line-height for readability)
- Step numbers: Figtree bold, large (`text-2xl font-bold text-amber-500`)
- Section headings: `font-heading text-2xl font-semibold text-blue-800`

### Urgent items
- Left border accent: `border-l-4 border-amber-500 pl-4`
- Background: `bg-amber-50`
- Icon: amber ExclamationCircle Heroicon

### Guide item checklist
- Completed-style step: blue check circle Heroicon (`text-blue-800`)
- Urgent step: amber exclamation circle Heroicon (`text-amber-500`)
- Item body text: `text-slate-600 text-sm leading-relaxed mt-1`

### CTA sections
- Referral CTA panel: `bg-blue-800 text-white rounded-xl p-8` with amber CTA button
- AI assistant hook: `bg-amber-50 border border-amber-200 rounded-xl p-6`

### Related organisations panel
- Card grid: `grid grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 gap-4`
- Org card: white rounded-xl with subtle blue-800 left border accent

### Tone guidance (content)
- Use "you" not "the user"
- Plain English — no jargon, no legal language
- Headings as questions where possible: "What help can I get?" not "Available Services"
- First step always: immediate/emergency contacts
